Core Themes

Struggle vs. Fate

Is life determined by an inescapable causality, or can human will carve its own path? Guts embodies the "struggle" against a predetermined, cruel destiny.

Humanity & Monstrosity

The series explores the thin line between man and monster, questioning whether true monstrosity lies in demonic form or in human ambition and betrayal.

Trauma & Healing

Characters are defined by their deep-seated trauma. The narrative examines if healing is possible and what form it takes in a world that constantly inflicts new wounds.
